# [Doc3] Objection Handling Scripts

**"It's too expensive."**
- *Response*: "I understand. Have you considered adjusting your deductible? Raising it from $500 to $1000 could drop your premium by 15%. Let's see how that looks."

**"I found a cheaper quote."**
- *Response*: "Does that quote include [Key Benefit like Accident Forgiveness]? Often teaser rates strip out coverage. Let's compare line-by-line to ensure you aren't underinsured."

**"I don't drive that much anymore."**
- *Response*: "We can look into a low-mileage discount or usage-based program. If you drive less than 7k miles, we can save you money right now."
