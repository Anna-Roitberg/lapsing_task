# [Doc1] Segment Messaging Strategy

**Segment A: The "Forgetful" (Good payer, sudden miss)**
- **Tone**: Friendly, Helpful.
- **Message**: "Oops! Looks like a payment slipped through. Click here to fix it."
- **Channel**: SMS / Email.

**Segment B: The "Price Shopper" (Complains about rate)**
- **Tone**: Value-oriented, educational.
- **Message**: "We reviewed your rates. You have [Feature] included, which most competitors charge extra for."
- **Channel**: Email (detailed comparison).

**Segment C: The "Distressed" (Financial hardship)**
- **Tone**: Empathetic, flexible.
- **Message**: "We're here to help. Let's look at payment options to keep you covered."
- **Channel**: Phone / Chat.
