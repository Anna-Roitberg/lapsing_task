# [Doc1] Grace Period & Billing Extensions

**Standard Grace Period**
- All policies have a **30-day grace period** from the due date.
- Coverage remains active during this time, but claims may be held pending payment.

**Intervention Timeline**
- **Day 1**: Missed Payment Notification (Email).
- **Day 7**: "Coverage at Risk" Warning (SMS + Email).
- **Day 15**: Urgent Reminder (Push Notification).
- **Day 25**: Final Notice / Lapse Warning (Direct Mail + Call for high value).

**Extension Requests**
- Agents can authorize a **one-time 10-day extension** beyond the grace period for hardship cases.
- Requires manager approval if the customer has lapsed in the last 12 months.
