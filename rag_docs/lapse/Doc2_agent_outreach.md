# [Doc2] Agent Outreach Protocols

**Trigger Criteria**
- Predicted Lapse Probability > 0.60.
- Premium > $150/month.
- Tenure > 2 years.

**Call Script Guidelines**
1. **Empathy First**: "I noticed a payment was missed and wanted to ensure you don't lose your tenure benefits."
2. **Value Reminder**: Mention specific used benefits (e.g., "You utilized the roadside assistance last year...").
3. **Solution Focus**: Immediately offer to fix it (Update card, switch date).

**Do Not Call**
- If customer has explicitly opted out of phone contact.
- If customer is in "Legal Collections" status.
