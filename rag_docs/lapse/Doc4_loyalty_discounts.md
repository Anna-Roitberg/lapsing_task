# [Doc4] Loyalty & Retention Discounts

**Eligibility**
- **Tenure > 2 Years**: Eligible for 5% loyalty discount applied to base premium.
- **Tenure > 5 Years**: Eligible for 10% Gold Member discount or Accident Forgiveness.

**Save-the-Sale Offers (Retention Desk Only)**
- **One-Month Waiver**: For customers with >5 years tenure threatening to cancel due to financial hardship. (Max 1 per lifetime).
- **Deductible Credit**: Reduce deductible by $100 for every year of claim-free driving/tenure.

**Positioning**
- "Because you've been with us since [Year], we can unlock this special rate."
